Welcome to...

Cash Mill Music Studio – a space where creativity and passion blend seamlessly to bring your musical vision to life. Located in the heart of RIverdale, our studio is equipped with state-of-the-art recording technology and designed to foster an inspiring environment for artists of all genres. Whether you're an up-and-coming musician, an established producer, or someone looking to make their first demo, we are here to provide you with professional services tailored to your needs. From music production and recording to mixing and mastering, our team of experienced engineers and producers work closely with you to ensure every note, beat, and melody shines through. At Cash Mill Music Studio, we believe that music is more than just sound – it’s a form of expression, a journey, and a story waiting to be told. We are passionate about helping you create music that resonates with your audience and reflects your unique style.

What We Offer:

  • High-Quality Recording: Professional recording equipment and acoustically treated rooms for pristine sound capture.
  • Music Production: Full production services, from arrangement to final mix, with expert guidance and support.
  • Mixing and Mastering: Industry-standard mixing and mastering to ensure your tracks sound polished and radio-ready.
  • Voice-over Recording: Perfect for podcasts, audiobooks, commercial voiceovers, and more.
  • Music Composition: Custom compositions tailored to your specific needs.
